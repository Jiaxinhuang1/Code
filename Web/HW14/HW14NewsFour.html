One of the Longest Anime of All Times: Doraemon

Doraemon is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Fujiko Fujio and first serialized in December 1969.
The story revolves around a robotic cat named Doraemon who travels back in time to aid a boy named Nobita Nobi.
It has 1,787 episodes and 30 specials.
