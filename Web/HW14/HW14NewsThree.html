Pokemon on its Seventh Series!

Staring from its Original Series at 1997, the show Pokemon went through drastic changes to keep up with the revolving society and advancing technology.
In 2002, Advanced Generations was released.
In 2006, Diamond and Pearl was released.
In 2010, Black and White was released.
In 2013, XY was released.
In 2016, Sun and Moon was released.
In 2019, Journeys was released.
